What are we doing and the mission statement

Executive Summary

The RENTIS Fund is a financial service company that focuses on stocks of environmentally responsible companies. The RENTIS Fund uses financial research purchased from leading analytical think-tanks and in-house environmental responsibility analysis to make recommendations to clients.


Services

The RENTIS Fund has developed a criteria-based marker system which is easy and effective in evaluating a wide range of different companies on their environmental impact. Only financially prudent/performing companies are evaluated, ensuring that its recommendations make both financial and environmental sense.


Competitive Edge

The RENTIS Fund will leverage the proprietory evaluation system to quickly gain market share. The system is convenient and based on extensive research, providing a streamlined overview of the environmental performance of the companies.
